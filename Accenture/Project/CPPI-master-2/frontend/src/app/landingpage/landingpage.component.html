Crowd Powered Product Innovation is revolutionizing the way companies innovate. As innovation continues to be at the forefront of digital disruption, companies look to outsourcing for their next “big idea.”

Digital Workforce has been pioneering the concept of crowdsourced virtual garages that support a structured innovation process executed by ad-hoc distributed teams with transient participation. These distributed teams are formed from passionate crowd that would like to contribute in their passion areas as well bench crowd that have idle capacity while on the bench. Crowdsourced virtual garage teams follow a structured process on the platform to create a pipeline of new product opportunities for organizations through a repeatable design and prototype process inspired by design and lean thinking
